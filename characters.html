Player Characters

Meet the group which calls themselves Bad Company

Borgan

Daan de vries


Borgan is the arcane warrior of the group. He uses his magic to enhace the swings he makes with his weapons. Able to deal devastating critical hits and freeze his oponnets solid and creating magical clones of himself. However Borgan is not actually very strong, for his magical powers have a sinister origin.

Bertha

Luna Tran


Bertha is the unexpected assasin of the group. She has a decieving character appearing nice, innocent and cute. However when push comes to shove she would not flinch to kill her target, unseen from the shadows. Occasionaly she uses poisoned ammunition when her situation seems dire or to catch the target alive.

Gale

Stijn lubbinge


Gale is aan experimetal pyromacer. Even though Gale knows other types of spells, he's primarely inclined to set his adversaries ablaze at the sight of them. Sometimes, he tends to get overexited with his fire magic, this often results into literal friendly fire.

Sicilia

Rhys timmermans

Sicilia is the herbalist and mystic of the group. He has multiple entities in his head that he can awaken for power and knowledge. Even though he remains on the background a lot he has already guided the group towards interresting adventures.

Liliana

Danse kleinluchtenbeld

Liliana is the trickster and the pranker of the group. Able to alter her appearance at will. After being rescued by the group when her whole party had disappeared. Having nowhere to go, she decided to travel with the group. This decision would later turn into a lifechanging revelation for her.

T1-NK3-R5

Halil ayden

T1-NK3-R5, also known as Tinker, is a warforged found by the group. After the group freed him from his hibernating state, he became the groups protector. Traveling with the group gives Tinker the oppertunity to find out more about himself since his past and memories are locked away in his head.

Velicia

Marsel galiev

Velicia is the main support of the group. She primeraly focusses on enhancing herself and her allies. Protecing them from charm magic and keeping them alive. Even though her primary focus is supporting her alies she has a small amount of powerfull damaging spells.
